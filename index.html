About $MPT

$MPT is a utility token designed for a play-and-earn ecosystem where users can mine tokens directly from the website and earn rewards by playing games. Unlike traditional mining, $MPT focuses on engagement-based mining, making it fun and accessible to everyone. Whether you're a casual gamer or a crypto enthusiast, you can accumulate and use $MPT for in-game rewards, NFT purchases, and more.
